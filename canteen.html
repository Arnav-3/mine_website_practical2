SWAADISH KHANA







1. SAMOSA

Samosa is a popular snack food in South Asian cuisine, particularly in India. It is a triangular-shaped pastry made of a thin flour dough filled with a mixture of spices, vegetables, or meat and then fried or baked until crispy. Samosas are often served with chutney or dipping sauce.

2. MOMOS

Momos are a type of dumpling popular in South Asian countries, especially in Tibet and Nepal. They are usually made with a thin dough wrapper filled with a mixture of seasoned minced meat, vegetables, or cheese, and steamed until cooked through. Momos are often served with a spicy dipping sauce and are a popular street food in many South Asian regions.

3. PIZZA

Pizza is a popular dish originating from Italy, made with a round flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and spices. The cheese is usually mozzarella, but other types of cheese can be used as well. Pizzas can be cooked in a wood-fired oven, but are more commonly baked in a conventional oven. Pizza is a versatile dish that comes in many different varieties, with toppings ranging from classic margherita to more adventurous combinations.

4. SPRING ROLL

Spring roll is a type of snack food that is commonly found in East and Southeast Asian cuisines. It typically consists of a filling, such as vegetables, meat, or seafood, that is wrapped in a thin flour or rice paper skin and then fried or baked. Spring rolls are often served as an appetizer or a snack and are sometimes served with a dipping sauce.
